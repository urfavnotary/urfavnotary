About

I am a certified and commissioned Notary Public providing quality service with professionalism and accuracy. My mission is to ensure your documents are handled properly and securely.

A California notary public is a state-appointed official who serves as an impartial witness to deter fraud by verifying the identity of signers and ensuring they are signing documents willingly. They are commissioned by the California Secretary of State and are authorized to perform notarial acts on legal documents like real estate deeds, powers of attorney, and living trusts.
